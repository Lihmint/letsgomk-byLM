Greg Oreskovich

Never settling for the expected, Greg has earned his creative stripes by starting every project from the understanding that no one looks forward to the next commercial or branded post on instagram. Thats why every idea brought into the room must hit on all cylinders. Entertaining. Surprising. Memorable. Worthy of a person’s limited attention span. From Gatorade to Porsche to Walt Disney World, he’s followed this mantra, collaborating with clients, strategists, filmmakers, photographers, influencers and fellow creatives who also believe — to make a positive difference for a brand, and occasionally even the world, begins with an idea that’s hard to forget. Greg lives by a simple philosophy. Life is more fun when you’re nice to everyone.

Monica Klasa

Brand builder. Visual Artist. Strategic Thinker. These are just a few of the talents Monica brings to the table for every client with whom she collaborates. With a rare ability to transform complex marketing challenges into solutions that are as beautiful as they are simple, it’s no surprise the world’s largest and most successful brands (Pepsico, Samsung, Unilever Beauty, Macy’s, Jergens, Lands End, Botox Beauty, Illinois Tourism to name a few) so confidently turn the visual reins of their brand over to her. From tourism brands to fashion and consumer goods, her impeccable design sense, unique style and 20+ years of experience as Creative Director develops brands target audiences fall in love with.
